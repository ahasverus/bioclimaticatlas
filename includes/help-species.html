Observations
Observed distribution of a species based on extent of occurrence polygons (source: BirdLife International, NatureServe and IUCN)
Probabilities
Probabilities of occurrence of a species predicted by niche models (values averaged over 70 predictions for 1981-2010 or 350 predictions for future periods for one RCP)
Binaries
Probabilities of occurrence of a species predicted by niche models converted in presence/absence data
Uncertainties
Standard deviations of probabilities of occurrence calculated over 70 values for 1981-2010 or 350 values for future periods for one RCP
