Species richness
Potential number of species for which niche models project suitable climate conditions
Species losses (%)
Potential proportion of species for which climate conditions may become unsuitable in the future compared to the baseline period
Species gains (%)
Potential proportion of species for which climate conditions may become suitable in the future compared to the baseline period
Species turnover
A measure of dissimilarity in species composition between future and the baseline period. A value of 0 indicates that the future and the baseline period communities may contain exactly the same species. A value of 1 indicates that the community may be completely different.
